Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access your datasets?

Some of our datasets are released openly through repositories like Mendeley Data and Mozilla Data Collective. Check the Publications page for links, or email us if you need guidance.

Do you offer student supervision or internships?

Yes. We mentor undergraduate and postgraduate students in NLP/ASR, data collection, annotation, and evaluation. Email us with your CV and a short description of your interests.

Can you help integrate Nupe/Gbagyi ASR or MT into an app?

We can explore research-to-deployment partnerships depending on scope, timeline, and data availability. Send us a short project brief and we’ll respond.

How can I contribute language data?

We welcome contributions from native speakers and community partners. We prioritize informed consent, privacy, and fair credit. Email us to discuss guidelines and formats.

Do you collaborate internationally?

Yes. We collaborate with researchers and communities globally, especially where partnerships strengthen ethical data creation and open, reusable tools for low-resource and endangered languages.
