Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access your research datasets?

Most of our datasets are freely available for research purposes. Please visit our GitHub repository or contact us for access credentials. Commercial use may require a separate license.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

Yes! We regularly host undergraduate and graduate interns. Applications are typically accepted twice a year (January and July). Check our website for current openings.

Can you help with Hausa language integration in my app?

We offer consulting services for organizations looking to integrate Hausa NLP capabilities. Contact our partnerships team to discuss your specific needs and requirements.

How can I contribute Hausa language data?

We welcome data contributions from native speakers and institutions. All contributors are properly credited. Please reach out to discuss data quality requirements and contribution processes.

Do you collaborate internationally?

Absolutely! We have active collaborations with institutions in Europe, North America, and other African countries. We're always open to new international partnerships.
