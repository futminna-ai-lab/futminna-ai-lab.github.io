Meet Our Team

FUT Minna AI Lab brings together researchers, engineers, and language experts working on low-resource and endangered language NLP, with a focus on Nupe (and endangered dialects), Gbagyi, multilingual MT, ASR, and Hausa code-switching.

Umar Baba Umar

Principal Investigator (PI)
PhD student in Computer Science, Federal University of Technology Minna (FUT Minna), Nigeria. Research focus: NLP for low-resource African languages, speech data collection, ASR, and code-switching. Experienced in community-driven dataset creation and facilitation on large-scale speech projects in Nigeria.

Dr. Abdulmalik Mohammed

Co-Principal Investigator (Co-PI)
Senior Lecturer in Computer Science, FUT Minna. PhD (University of Manchester). Expert in AI/ML and speech/language technologies. Completed an AI Research Fellowship at Microsoft (2025), working on speech recognition for low-resource languages. Works on small language models, ASR, and TTS.
