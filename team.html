Meet Our Team

FUT Minna AI Lab brings together researchers, engineers, and language experts working on low-resource and endangered language NLP, with a focus on multilingual MT, ASR, and code-switching.

PhD student in Computer Science, Federal University of Technology Minna (FUT Minna), Nigeria. Research focus: NLP for low-resource African languages, speech data collection, ASR, and code-switching. Experienced in community-driven dataset creation and facilitation on large-scale speech projects in Nigeria.
