Research & Development

Exploring the frontiers of Hausa language technology through innovative research and practical applications.

Our Research Areas

HausaNLP focuses on developing comprehensive language technologies that address the unique challenges of processing Hausa text and speech. Our interdisciplinary approach combines traditional linguistic knowledge with modern AI techniques.

Machine Translation

Building robust translation systems between Hausa and major world languages, enabling global communication and knowledge sharing. Our neural models achieve state-of-the-art performance for Hausa translation pairs.

Language Models

Training large-scale language models specifically for Hausa, capturing the nuances and cultural context of the language. Our HausaGPT model shows promising results in text generation and understanding.

Speech Recognition

Developing accurate speech-to-text systems that understand Hausa dialects and regional variations across different communities. Our ASR systems work effectively in noisy, real-world environments.

Sentiment Analysis

Creating tools to analyze emotions and opinions in Hausa text for social media monitoring and market research applications. Our models understand cultural context and linguistic subtleties.

Corpus Development

Building comprehensive datasets and linguistic resources to support research and development in Hausa NLP applications. Our corpora include diverse genres, dialects, and domains.

Educational Tools

Developing AI-powered learning platforms and tools to support Hausa language education and literacy programs. Our adaptive systems personalize learning experiences.

HausaBERT

Our flagship pre-trained language model for Hausa, based on the BERT architecture but specifically optimized for Hausa morphology and syntax. HausaBERT achieves state-of-the-art performance on downstream tasks including named entity recognition, part-of-speech tagging, and text classification.

  • View Model

Hausa Speech Corpus

The largest publicly available Hausa speech dataset, containing over 1000 hours of annotated speech from diverse speakers across Nigeria and Niger. This resource is enabling breakthrough research in Hausa automatic speech recognition and text-to-speech synthesis.

  • Access Dataset

Morphological Analyzer

A comprehensive computational tool for Hausa morphological analysis, capable of parsing complex word formations and generating morphological variants. This tool is essential for many NLP applications and linguistic research.
